WayToClinic - Comprehensive Health Solutions

WayToClinic™ Solutions is a kaptanganj, Uttar Pradesh-headquartered start-up focused on providing high-quality in-person consultation and teleconsultation based healthcare services. As of today, we have established a state-of-the-art physical homeopathy treatment clinic in kaptanganj as well as a dedicated teleconsultation center to cater to patients pan India.

Our mission and vision is to provide world-class homeopathic healthcare to every corner of India via a two-fold approach of physical clinics and tele-medicine. 

WayToClinic™ Solutions boasts of having under its aegis a strong team consisting highly-qualified homeopath doctors and paramedical staff who work dedicatedly and round-the-clock to provide physical and online consultations. Besides providing online consultation through tele-medicine facility, we also do doorstep delivery of medicines to patients across India.

With 97% cure ratio of our pateints, WayToClinic™ Solutions is creating an unparalleled credibility and reputation amongst the masses, while providing treatment of an array of diseases and disorders – starting from PCOD/PCOS, piles, fistula, fissure to arthritis, spondylitis, renal stone, gout, male sexual disorders, and many others.

WayToClinic™’s main USP lies in tech-enabled, new-age paperless clinic operations. The company was started by husband-wife entrepreneur duo Alok Katiyar and Dr. Deeksha Katiyar back in 2020, who had noticed some gaps in the homeopathic healthcare market.

They saw that despite homeopathy gaining traction in India, the existing doctors were only serving a smaller population through physical clinics, and also that there were no large players in this domain as well as a lack of availability of modern homeopathy clinics across towns and cities in India. In a bid to change the scenario for the better and to provide affordable-yet-superlative healthcare for a billion people in India, WayToClinic™ was born. 

WayToClinic™ Homeopathy is currently serving 12,000+ patients every month, and growing its business at 10% Month-on-Month (MOM). Having already served over 1 lakh patients since its inception, including those from metro cities and tier-1 cities to those residing in the remotest villages and nooks and corners of Uttarakhand, Himachal Pradesh and other states, WayToClinic™ is constantly growing its reputation and patient base year-on-year.
