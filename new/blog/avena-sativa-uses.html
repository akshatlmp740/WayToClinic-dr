Avena Sativa Mother Tincture

The mother tincture is the first step in preparing homeopathic medicines. It is made by macerating the fresh plant material in alcohol. The mother tincture is then used to make dilutions.

  • Steps of preparation of mother tincture
    • Collect fresh Avena Sativa plants.
    • Wash the plants thoroughly and chop them into small pieces.
    • Place the chopped plant material in a clean glass jar.
    • Add enough alcohol to cover the plant material.
    • Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-4 weeks.
    • Shake the jar daily.
    • After 2-4 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle.
    • The mother tincture is now ready to use.
  • Method of administration:
    • The mother tincture can be used to make dilutions or taken directly. To take it directly, place a few drops under the tongue and hold them there for 30 seconds before swallowing. You can take it 2-3 times per day.
    • Add a few drops of the mother tincture to a water bottle to make dilutions. The potency of the dilution will depend on the ratio of mother tincture to water. For example, a 1:10 dilution is made by adding 1 drop of mother tincture to 9 drops of water.
